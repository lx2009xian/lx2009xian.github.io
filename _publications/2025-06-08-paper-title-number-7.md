This study revisits the **mathematical foundations of Triple Helix synergy** measurement using **information theory**.  
The authors provide a refined understanding of how mutual information among universities, industries, and governments can be decomposed and interpreted, thus strengthening the theoretical basis for studying innovation system dynamics.

### Key Contributions
- Offers **mathematical clarification** for Triple Helix entropy and synergy calculations.  
- Provides a **theoretical correction** to improve comparability and validity of Triple Helix metrics.
