This paper proposes a **quantitative model** for describing how scientific citations evolve over time.  
Using data across multiple disciplines, the study formulates and tests **time-dependent mathematical functions** that capture typical citation growth and decay behaviors.

### Key Contributions
- Introduces **time-based mathematical functions** to model citation dynamics.
